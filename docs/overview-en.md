# Chronnote Overview

Chronnote is a local-first knowledge workspace for deep reading, structured notes, PDFs, whiteboards, todos, and AI-assisted thinking.

It is designed for people who work with real materials over time: papers, documents, ideas, tasks, decisions, and the questions that connect them.

## What Chronnote Helps With

- Keep notes, PDFs, whiteboards, and todos in one connected workspace
- Read and annotate long documents without losing context
- Build backlinks and references between materials
- Turn scattered information into durable thinking structures
- Ask AI to reason with your actual materials, not an empty prompt

## Product Philosophy

Chronnote follows the loop of question -> thought -> insight.

The goal is not to generate more text for its own sake. The goal is to help you ask better questions, preserve the context behind your answers, and return to your thinking later.
